FAQ

Common questions about ordering, delivery and customization

How do I order?

Add products to cart → Checkout → Send order via WhatsApp. We confirm stock, delivery time and payment details.

Do you deliver islandwide?

Yes. Islandwide delivery is available. Delivery times and charges (if any) are confirmed after order.

Can I choose sizes and colors?

Yes. Each product page shows available sizes and colors. Message us for custom options.

Do you have a showroom?

Not currently. Orders are handled online via WhatsApp and phone.

How do payments work?

Payments are arranged after you contact us (bank transfer / COD where applicable).

Still have a question? Message us on WhatsApp and we’ll help you quickly.
